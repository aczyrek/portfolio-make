Task Automation & AI

Centralized AI Task Management System

Advanced system automatically capturing unstructured notes and tasks from Google Tasks. It utilizes the capabilities of the Gemini 2.5 Flash language model for intelligent context understanding, priority assignment, and appropriate categorization (Work/Home/Other).

Challenge Scattered tasks and lack of consistent prioritization, forcing manual "triage".
Solution A pipeline in Make.com with integrated AI. Structured tasks are created in Todoist, the entire process logic is logged for security in Airtable, and the operation's success is reported to the team on Slack.
Result Saving ~10h weekly on task management, full error-free structuring of the backlog.
